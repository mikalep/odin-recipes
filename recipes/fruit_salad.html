Delicious Fruit Salad

Delicious fruit salad consisting of multiple different fruits.

Ingredients

  • 1 pint of strawberries - cleaned, hulled and sliced
  • 1 pound of seedless grapes, halved
  • 3 kiwis, peeled and sliced
  • 3 bananas, sliced
  • 2 apples, sliced
  • 1 can peach pie filling

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients
  2. Get a knife and a cutting board
  3. Slice up all the needed fruits
  4. Take a big and clean bowl
  5. Throw all fruits into the bowl
  6. Throw in the peach pie filling
  7. Mix it up!
  8. Enjoy!
