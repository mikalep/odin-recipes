Gin Tonic

The one and only GT

One of the most iconic drinks known to man, and it's so simple!

Ingredients

  • 4 cubes of ice
  • 2 (or more) fluid ounces of gin
  • 4 fluid ounces of tonic water
  • 1 tablespoon of fresh lime juice
  • 1 lime wedge

Steps

  1. Take out your glass and a bottle of gin
  2. Throw the ice cubes into the glass
  3. Pour in the gin
  4. Toss in the lime juice
  5. Mix it up!
  6. Put in the lime wedge
  7. Enjoy!
  8. Make another.. and another..
